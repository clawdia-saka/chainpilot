# ChainPilot Demo Video Script

## Narration (English, gTTS)
Duration target: 2-3 minutes

---

### Scene 1: Hook (0-15s)
**Screen:** Chain logos scattered, USDC fragmented across chains
**Voice:**
"DeFi yield is fragmented. Your stablecoins sit on one chain while better rates exist on another. Manually bridging and rebalancing is slow, risky, and expensive."

### Scene 2: Solution (15-30s)
**Screen:** ChainPilot dashboard overview, architecture diagram
**Voice:**
"Meet ChainPilot — an autonomous cross-chain yield allocator. It scans yields across protocols and chains, makes allocation decisions, bridges via LI.FI, and monitors for anomalies. All in one cycle."

### Scene 3: Live Cycle — Intel (30-50s)
**Screen:** Pipeline lights up "Intel" step, log scrolling
**Voice:**
"First, the Intel module scans eight yield endpoints. Ethena s-U-S-D-e offers three point five percent. Aave USDC on Base offers two point seven. ChainPilot picks the best risk-adjusted allocation."

### Scene 4: Live Cycle — Bridge (50-80s)
**Screen:** Pipeline lights up "Bridge", LI.FI logo prominent
**Voice:**
"Next, ChainPilot bridges USDC from Ethereum to Base and Arbitrum using LI.FI. The aggregator finds the optimal route — in this case, Mayan protocol. Settlement takes just thirty-four seconds."

### Scene 5: Live Cycle — Result (80-100s)
**Screen:** Chain cards update with sUSDe balances
**Voice:**
"Done. Ten dollars and sixty-six cents of USDC is now allocated across three chains. Two point eight s-U-S-D-e on Base. Two point five on Arbitrum. Four dollars remaining on Ethereum. All earning yield."

### Scene 6: Sentinel (100-130s)
**Screen:** Sentinel monitor section, health cards
**Voice:**
"The Sentinel module continuously monitors protocol health. It checks T-V-L with a three-tier fallback — on-chain data, exchange rate conversion, and DeFi Llama API. If T-V-L drops ten percent or a depeg exceeds two percent, ChainPilot automatically evacuates to USDC and redeploys to a healthy protocol."

### Scene 7: Backtest (130-150s)
**Screen:** Backtest comparison chart
**Voice:**
"Over twenty-four months of backtesting, ChainPilot achieved nine point eight percent effective A-P-Y — turning ten thousand dollars into twelve thousand sixty. The real alpha isn't just yield — it's anomaly avoidance."

### Scene 8: Closing (150-170s)
**Screen:** Overview with LI.FI badge, links
**Voice:**
"ChainPilot. Cross-chain yield allocation, powered by LI.FI. Built for the Vibeathon twenty twenty-six."

---

## Production Notes
- gTTS: English, slow=False
- Background music: lo-fi or ambient (optional, low volume)
- Each scene = series of browser screenshots or dashboard recording
- Total narration: ~170 seconds (~2:50)
